1st Annual Puerto Vallarta Independent Film Festival
Puerto Vallarta, Jalisco, Mexico
October 8th & 9th 2022
We are proud to annouce the first installment of the Puerto Vallarta Independent Film Festival, a celebration of audiovisual artistry that seeks to unearth and elevate the magic of independent film and its creators

We invite you to bear witness to the sights and sounds, the beauty and wonder, the apprenticeship and mastery, the thrill and immersion, the vigor and derangement, and the imposition and atonement of modern independent film - and to recognize the writers, creators, directors, producers, actors, and executives who bring life to the screen.

The 1st Annual Puerto Vallarta Independent Film Festival will be the staging ground for both broadcasted and never-before-seen releases of feature-length independent films from all over Mexico, representing a multitude of cultures, languages, and background. Thanks to the generous support of our sponsors, Chazz™ and Usher™, we are able to open our doors so that the public may become an audience to this event and the films that it screens.
